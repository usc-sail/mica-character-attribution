Instructions

A trope is a storytelling device or convention the storyteller uses to describe situations the audience can easily recognize, often used to stereotype characters. Think of them as character attributes.

Your task is as follows:

  1. Identify the movie character from the pictures and the given movie(s). If you are having trouble recognizing the character, click on the Character Page link(s).
  2. Read the definition of the character trope. You can open the link to know more.
  3. Choose yes, maybe yes, not sure, maybe no, or no to answer whether the character portrays or is associated with the trope.
  4. (Optional) Explain your choice in the provided text area or leave any other comment behind.

You can read about the character online in sites such as Wikipedia or Fandom Wikis. We provide the links to the Wikipedia movie page and the character fandom page whenever possible. Creating an account at the Fandom website can reduce the advertisements you see when visiting a Fandom page.

We strongly discourage the use of Large Language Models such as Gemini, Claude, and ChatGPT to answer the questions. These models can hallucinate and you must check the veracity of their responses.

Do not speed through the HITs and take sufficient time to understand the character and the trope before answering. The HITs contain test questions that look identical to regular questions. We will disqualify workers who perform poorly on these test questions or if they spam their answers.

Do not hoard too many HITs to yourself. We recommend accepting not more than 50 HITs so that all qualified workers can get the chance to work.
